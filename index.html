How to play

The purpose of the game is to successfully finish a lunch session at your restaurant.
You will play as the the waiter. Keep the costumers happy so that they leave good reviews of our restaurant on TripAdvisor!

Assign tables to each costumer that arrives by clicking on those that are empty.

  • An empty table will be painted green,
  • an occupied table with costumers eating will appear yellow, and
  • a table with costumers that are finished eating will look red.

Costumers will leave the table free once you collect their bill by clicking on the table.
Stay alert so that costumers don't lose their patience (on the queue or waiting to pay).
If you make it through the whole rush hour (1 minute), you will become champion!
